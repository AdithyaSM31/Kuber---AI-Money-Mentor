- debtHealth: EMI-to-income ratio.
  No loans=100, <20%=80, 20-30%=60, 30-40%=35, >40%=10
- taxEfficiency: Based on regime + SIP.
  Old regime + ELSS possible = score based on utilization estimate.
  New regime=60 (simpler but fewer deductions). Unsure=30.
- retirement: Based on age and SIP rate.
  Under 30 with any SIP=60+. Over 40 with no SIP=10.
